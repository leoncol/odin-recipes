Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish consisting of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, typically flavored with soy sauce or miso, and topped with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (wheat flour, water, salt, kansui)
  • Broth (varies by type: shoyu, miso, shio, tonkotsu)
  • Chashu (braised or roasted pork)
  • Negi (thinly sliced green onions)
  • Menma (fermented bamboo shoots)
  • Nori (dried seaweed)
  • Ajitsuke Tamago (soft-boiled marinated egg)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add noodles and cook until tender (follow package instructions).
  3. Prepare broth by simmering broth with soy sauce or miso paste for flavor.
  4. Slice chashu pork into thin pieces and chop green onions into small rings.
  5. Reheat menma (bamboo shoots) if using, and cut nori (seaweed) into strips.
  6. Peel and slice ajitsuke tamago (marinated soft-boiled egg).
  7. Divide cooked noodles into serving bowls.
  8. Pour hot broth over noodles.
  9. Add chashu pork, green onions, menma, nori, and ajitsuke tamago on top.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade ramen!
