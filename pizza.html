Pizza

Pizza is a popular dish of Italian origin consisting of a typically round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients such as meat, vegetables, and condiments.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Basil (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, water, yeast, and salt to form the dough.
  2. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature setting (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  8. Add optional toppings like garlic and basil if desired.
  9. Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
